  <div class="post-content">

The Scrolls of Dreadmere is a collection of writings from deep within the realm — fan fiction, original adventures, and chronicles of the worlds that matter most.

You'll find stories set in the worlds of Beast Quest, How to Train Your Dragon, and beyond. You'll find original characters facing original monsters. You'll find biographies of villains, histories of forgotten kingdoms, and tales told in the old way — long, detailed, and unwilling to rush to the end.

The scribe has been writing since before the quill knew how to stop.

## What Lives Here

- **Fan Fiction** — new stories in worlds already beloved
- **Original Adventures** — characters and realms born entirely within Dreadmere
- **The Chronicles** — biographies, lore, histories, and compendiums

## The Name

*Dreadmere* is a place. Dark waters, deep roots, things that move beneath the surface. The scribe lives there. This is where the writing comes from.

---

*The Scrolls of Dreadmere is a personal writing archive. All original characters, plots, and worldbuilding belong to their respective authors. Fan fiction is written for love of the source material, not profit.*
